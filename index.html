About Us

Fit N’ Fresh Personal Training is a results led company providing training solutions to the people of Cardiff and the Vale. Here at Fit N’ Fresh we strive to achieve greatness. Our passion for success is what drives and motivates us to turn your dreams into a reality. With consistent in app exercise tracking, we monitor your intensity, progression and personal bests in order to fulfil your goals. We offer one 2 one and semi personal training sessions in a gym, studio or outdoor environment. We also provide home and work visits.

Bio

Dan has been working in the fitness industry for some time now, and is a Level 3 Personal Trainer. He's an aspiring triathlete with a Level 1 coaching qualification under his belt.

Along with Dan’s triathlon coaching, he is also a qualified spin, kettlebell and circuit instructor covering various classes around Cardiff. Dan has experience training with various individuals ranging from your beginner gym user to the prop forward.

Dan's passion for excercise has led him to compete in various events around the country. Ranging from the SWYD Sprint Triathlon, Cardiff 10k and the Cardiff half marathon, with his biggest achievement to date being the Newport Marathon. Dan is eager to tackle the Iron Man Wales event in 2019.
